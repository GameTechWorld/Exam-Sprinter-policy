Privacy Policy

ExamSprint – Smart Exam Preparation App

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

ExamSprint (“we”, “us”, “our”) is an Android learning app that helps students and aspirants in India prepare for competitive exams using personalised mock tests, spaced repetition, and AI-powered doubt solving.

ExamSprint is operated by Saiven (proprietorship), an independent developer based in India.
Registered Address: Delhi, India

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal data when you use ExamSprint, in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (“DPDP Act”) and other applicable Indian laws.

Our Privacy Commitments
• We collect only the minimum data required for core features.
• AI doubt queries are processed ephemerally and are not used to train models.
• Learning attempt logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.
• We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
• You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time.

1. Scope & Applicability

This policy applies to all users of the ExamSprint mobile application, website, and related services, wherever they are located, as long as we process their personal data in digital form. [It does not apply to purely offline use or anonymised data that can no longer identify you.]

2. Information We Collect

We only access basic profile information (name, email address, and profile picture) through Google Sign-In and do not access your contacts, Google Drive, or any other personal data from your Google account.

Ads displayed in the app may be personalized based on limited device signals provided by Google, or may be shown as non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and applicable consent requirements.

2A. Tracking Technologies

We use third-party SDKs such as Firebase and Google AdMob that function similarly to cookies to collect usage data, device information, and interaction signals for analytics, performance monitoring, security, and advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate personalised mock tests
  • To provide AI-powered explanations
  • To manage subscriptions
  • To improve app security, detect misuse, abuse, or unfair practices (including automated scraping or exam-cheating attempts), and fix bugs

4. Legal Basis & Consent Under DPDP Act

Under the DPDP Act, we process your personal data mainly on the basis of your consent and for certain legitimate purposes permitted by law (such as fraud prevention, network security, and compliance with legal obligations).

  • Consent: When you first sign in to ExamSprint, we present a clear privacy notice and request your consent for processing your personal data. By continuing to use the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw consent at any time from the in-app Settings or by emailing us. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing already carried out, but may limit some features.
  • Legitimate Uses: Even if you withdraw consent, we may retain and process limited data for legal compliance, fraud detection, billing disputes, and security purposes as allowed by law.

5. Your Rights

As a Data Principal under the DPDP Act, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a summary of your personal data that we process.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction or updating of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw your consent for further processing at any time.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer if you are dissatisfied with our response.

You can exercise these rights by emailing us at Examsprintersupport@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 30 days or within the timelines prescribed by law.

6. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We rely on trusted third-party service providers (data processors) to run ExamSprint. They are contractually bound to process your data only on our instructions, implement appropriate security measures, and not sell your data.

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, analytics, crash reporting, device telemetry.
  • Supabase: Secure storage of learning logs, spaced-repetition data, and subscription metadata.
  • Google Gemini: AI-powered question generation and doubt solving. Your queries are processed ephemerally and are not used to train general models.
  • Google AdMob: In-app advertisements for non-premium users. We do not directly share your name, email, or test data with advertisers.
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription and payment processing for Ranker’s Pass.

We may also share data when required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect our legal rights, users, or the public.

7. International Data Transfers

When we transfer your personal data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required under the DPDP Act. These safeguards may include standard contractual clauses and reliance on trusted service providers with globally recognised compliance and security frameworks.

8. Data Retention

Logs are deleted within ~30 days.

Account deletion requests are processed within 7–30 days, including removal from active systems and backups where feasible.

9. Automated Processing

Used for recommendations and scheduling.

These systems do not affect your eligibility for exams, employment, financial opportunities, or legally significant outcomes.

10. Children’s Privacy

ExamSprint is intended for students aged 16 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent.

If you are under 13, you must not use ExamSprint or submit any personal data to us. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, we will promptly delete such data and may disable the associated account.

For users between 13 and 18, we encourage the involvement and supervision of parents or guardians when using the app.

11. Security

AI-generated responses may occasionally be inaccurate or incomplete. Users should independently verify important information.

We do not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free service.

12. Copyright & Use of Educational Content

ExamSprint generates primarily original questions and explanations using a combination of artificial intelligence (including Google Gemini) and human review and editing. We take steps to minimise similarity with existing question banks.

  • Prompts are crafted to create fresh, exam-relevant content tailored to Indian competitive exams.
  • Questions may be checked using search tools and internal checks for potential similarity.
  • Where necessary, questions are substantially rewritten (Indian context, new data, options, or formats).
  • Human reviewers may further edit questions for clarity and creativity.

Individual questions and factual content may not always be protected by copyright; however, our curated collections, structure, and edited content generally are. You may use questions and explanations for your personal learning only. Commercial use, large-scale scraping, or redistribution of our content without prior written permission is prohibited.

If you believe any content on ExamSprint infringes your rights, please contact us with details of your claim. We will investigate and, where appropriate, modify or remove the material.

13. Data Safety & App Store Disclosures

We complete and maintain the Google Play Store “Data Safety” form to accurately describe how ExamSprint collects, uses, and shares user data. Providing false or incomplete information can lead to app removal, so we keep this disclosure aligned with our actual data flows.

In summary, we:

  • Collect personal info (name, email), app activity (test attempts, AI queries), and app diagnostics (crash logs).
  • Encrypt data in transit using HTTPS.
  • Support account and data deletion from within the app.
  • Do not sell your data to third parties.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our services. When we make material changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice, email, or website banner at least 7 days before the new policy takes effect, where required.

Your continued use of ExamSprint after the updated policy becomes effective will indicate your acceptance of the changes.
