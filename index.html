Privacy Policy

ExamSprint – Smart Exam Preparation App

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

ExamSprint (“we”, “us”, “our”) is an Android app dedicated to helping students and aspirants in India excel in competitive exams through personalized mock tests, spaced repetition, and AI-powered doubt solving.

We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Our Commitment: We collect only the minimum data necessary to deliver a high-quality learning experience. Your AI doubt queries are processed ephemerally and never used to train models. Attempt logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.

1. Information We Collect

Category Specific Data Source Purpose
Profile Information Name, email, profile photo URL Google Sign-In (Firebase Auth) Account creation & login
Learning Data Test scores, attempted questions, time taken, accuracy User activity (Supabase) Personalised analytics & progress tracking
Spaced Repetition Data Item ID, recall quality (0–5) User activity SM-2 algorithm for review scheduling
AI Doubt Solver Data Text queries, uploaded images User input (Google Gemini) Real-time doubt clearing
Device & App Data OS version, device model, locale, root status Device sensors (Firebase Analytics) Security, crash reporting, optimisation
Purchase Data Subscription status, Play Store receipt tokens Google Play Billing + Supabase Managing “Ranker’s Pass” premium access

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised mock tests and performance insights
  • To schedule spaced-repetition reviews using the SM-2 algorithm
  • To provide instant AI-powered doubt explanations
  • To send helpful motivational nudges (Momentum Loss / Score Anxiety alerts)
  • To manage your “Ranker’s Pass” subscription and access control
  • To improve app security and fix bugs (via anonymised analytics)

We never sell your personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted providers:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Supabase – Secure storage of your learning logs
  • Google Gemini – AI-powered question generation and doubt solving (queries are not used for model training)
  • Google AdMob – Non-intrusive ads for free (non-premium) users
  • Google Play Billing – Subscription management

All data is transferred using encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Learning/attempt logs → Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • AI queries → Deleted immediately after generating the response
  • Profile data → Kept until you delete your account

You can delete your account anytime from the Profile section in the app. Upon deletion, we will permanently remove your identity and all associated learning data within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or download your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics or personalised nudges in Settings
  • Request any other privacy-related action

Contact us at support@examsprint.com. We respond to all verifiable requests within 30 days.

6. Security

All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS with certificate pinning) and at rest. We regularly audit our systems and follow industry-standard security practices.

7. Children’s Privacy

ExamSprint is designed for students preparing for competitive exams (typically 16+ years old). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided data, please contact us immediately to delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you via in-app message or email at least 7 days before any material changes take effect. Your continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
